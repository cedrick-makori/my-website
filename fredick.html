Projects

Thermoelectric Cooler

Create a small cooler using thermoelectric modules to explore heat transfer and thermodynamics. This project involves building a compact system that can cool a small container, useful in understanding applications of heat exchange.

Hydraulic Arm Model

Build a simple hydraulic arm using syringes and tubing to demonstrate fluid mechanics principles. This project can help you learn about hydraulics, force distribution, and the basics of robotic arm movement.

Wind Turbine Design

Design and construct a miniature wind turbine to generate electricity. This project covers concepts in energy conversion, aerodynamics, and mechanical design, giving you experience with sustainable energy applications.

3D Printed Gearbox

Design and 3D print a small gearbox to understand the mechanics of gears and torque. This project combines design software skills with knowledge of materials and mechanical motion, providing hands-on experience with key engineering concepts.
